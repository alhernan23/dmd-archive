03: Interactive Experience

For this last project, we were to create a website with an interactive element using p5.js, and answering a creative question of our choice. I decided to explore the idea of how unfamiliar things can still be familiar because the world just recycles and reuses things, and we all have a sense of nostalgia in us. I also wanted to incoporate some storytelling not just through visuals, but also sound so I created some sound art pieces for this project. The project resulted in a sound art library/archive.
