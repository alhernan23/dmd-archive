02: Net Art Zine

In the next project of the semester, we were to create a net art zine using a few interactive elements and going with a theme of our choice. I chose the theme of "friendship," since my relationships with my high school friends are really important to me. In this zine, I got my friends to write their own pieces, and I encoded them so that the user would have to decipher the different "puzzles." I also got to feature my own original art work and music which was fun.
